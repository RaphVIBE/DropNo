## 5. The mechanism (reminder)

Buyers place a **sealed bid**, editable until one hour before the reveal. At the
reveal, the N highest bids at or above the floor win; **all winners pay the same
price**: that of the last winning bid (single clearing price). No public auction,
no real-time overbidding, no price war.

---

## 6. Commercial terms

- **Drop No. commission: 12 %** of the clearing price, per unit actually sold.
  _(No commission if the drop is cancelled below floor.)_
- **Buyer fees: 0 %** — the single displayed price is the price paid.
- The Maison **keeps control** of the floor price, the calendar and the number of
  units.

## 7. Logistics & insurance

The piece leaves the Maison's workshop only to reach a verified buyer, by
**insured door-to-door delivery** (insured DHL Express; hand-carried concierge
above 10,000 €). **Drop No. never holds the piece in stock.**

---

## 8. Buyers & authenticity

- Each buyer is **verified** (identity — Stripe Identity) and their card is
  **pre-authorised** before the reveal.
- Pieces are sold **new, direct from the Maison**, with its warranty and, where
  applicable, its certificate of authenticity. No resale, no second-hand.

---

## 9. Settlement to the Maison

The proceeds of sales (net of commission) are paid to the Maison after the
payments are captured and the buyer's legal withdrawal period (14 days) has
elapsed. Terms detailed in the final contract.
