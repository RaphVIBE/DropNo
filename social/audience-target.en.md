# Drop № — Audience and targeting (decided 2026-06-12)

English version of `cible-audience.md`. The French file remains the source of truth for the team; this version exists to share with English-speaking partners, maisons and investors.

Founding document: who we speak to. Every caption, visual and platform choice is validated against this file. Complements `editorial-line.en.md`.

## The principle: three distinct audiences

We are not speaking to one person. Three circles, three channels, three goals. Never blur them inside a single post.

1. **The buyer** — the one who bids. Commercial core. Everything converges on him.
2. **The follower** — the one we attract on the feed during stealth. Funnel toward the buyer. Broader, but not unlimited: we attract the follower who *can become* a buyer, not the whole planet.
3. **The maison** — the one we want to sign. B2B. Dedicated channel: LinkedIn.

---

## 1. The buyer (primary persona) — "locked out of the system"

The client is neither the beginner nor the collector already served. He is the one with the means and the taste, but whom the traditional system leaves at the door.

**Profile**
- 32-48, urban (Brussels, Geneva, Paris, Lausanne, Antwerp).
- Comfortable income: senior manager, founder, professional, finance, tech. Often "new money" without the boutique-relationship history.
- Already owns 2 to 4 fine watches. Knows the value of things. Knows what a manufacture calibre is.
- Tech-comfortable: bids online without friction, passes KYC without worry.

**His pain (our way in)**
- The waiting-list wall: the piece he wants, they refuse to list him, or it is eight years.
- The grey market: he can have it now, but 40% over retail, no papers, no relationship.
- The humiliation of allocation: retail has become an audition he fails despite his money.

**What he wants**
- Fair access, not a backdoor. A chance he does not have today.
- Transparency: knowing the rules of the game in advance.
- To buy direct, a piece with papers, with no intermediary capturing the margin.

**What he rejects**
- Empty hype, bling, the lifestyle wristshot.
- Being talked to like a beginner.
- Opaque mechanics.

## 2. Emotional promise: ACCESS / FAIRNESS

The throughline of all communication, from the first stealth post to the reveal:

> We give you the chance the boutique denies you. Sealed bids, one fair price for all, zero allocation politics.

This is why the **Provenance** pillar (broken waiting lists, grey market, sealed sales, serial 001) is the heart of the seeding: every post about the injustice of today's system sets up the relief Drop № will offer. We do not sell the thrill of the drop (Supreme energy) or belonging alone: we sell the **end of a specific frustration**.

For every post, the test: "does this speak to someone who cannot buy the watch he wants?" If a post touches neither the knowledge, the taste, nor the access pain of the locked-out buyer, it is cut.

## 3. Geography: hyper-local BE/FR/CH first

We prove the model on a tight francophone core before expanding.

- **Launch market**: Belgium, France, French-speaking Switzerland. Founder proximity, accessible Romandy maisons, familiar EU regulatory frame.
- **Major consequence**: the target buyer is francophone, but the feed stays **in English** (decided 2026-06-12). Reason: the affluent francophone buyer consumes watch content in EN (#watchfam = lingua franca), EN carries the international premium and builds the audience faster. LinkedIn stays FR+EN (the maison is spoken to in its own language).
- **Expansion**: pan-EU English in v2, once the model is proven locally.

---

## 4. The maison (B2B audience, LinkedIn)

Target: independent maisons and mid-sized watchmakers, primarily French-speaking Switzerland and France, suffering the loss of control over their distribution.

- Decision-maker: founder, commercial director, retail/D2C lead.
- Their pain: watching the value of their pieces leak to the grey market, not knowing their end customer, depending on a network of retailers.
- Our B2B promise: regain control of the workshop-to-wrist link, sell direct, choose who receives the rare piece, keep the data and the relationship.
- LinkedIn tone: industry analysis, FR first (Romandy/French target), never consumer.

---

## Who the target is NOT

- The ultra-wealthy already served by boutiques (they do not have the pain).
- Pure hype-chasers without means (the €3,000 floor excludes them).
- Resellers / flippers (KYC and brand-direct B2C discourage them; C2C out of scope).
- The global English-speaking watch public at large, while we are in the hyper-local phase.
